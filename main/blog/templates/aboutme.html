About me:

My real name is Jesse Bruce Pinkman. I was born on September 24, 1984, into a wealthy family in Albuquerque, New Mexico. I had long been estranged from his parents due to his drug addiction and lifestyle. After I left my parents' house, I moved in with my aunt Ginny, whom I took care of until her death from cancer, after which I was allowed to stay in her house. I was a bad student at school and preferred to hang out with friends and take methamphetamine. Walter White, whom I almost always call "Mr. White," was my chemistry teacher. Mother of mine later recalled that "Walt must have seen some potential in Jesse; he was really trying to motivate him. He was one of the few teachers who took care of him."
